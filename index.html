Why is there a race car on my desk?

In 2025, my siblings and I got an amazing gift. The President of the FIA gave us VIP tickets to the Monza Grand Prix. We were right in the middle of the action.

I didn't just watch the race. I watched the people. The service was perfect. They knew what I wanted before I even asked. I realized that this is what "True Hospitality" means.

I am using this lesson right now. I am building a robot for events (see the Robot section!). I don't just want it to work; I want it to treat guests with that same perfect level of service I felt at Monza.

It wasn't just about the experience, it made me think about how something given for free could mean so much for someone. The tickets were a gift, but the experience was priceless. That's why, when I can, I give back to the world: you never know how much something you do for free could mean to someone else.

I also learned that the best moments in life are often unexpected. I never planned to go to the paddock for a full day, but it became one of the most inspiring days of my life.

I put this bin here to be honest with you. Not everything I touch turns to gold. But if it ain't gold, it is a lesson I will never forget.
