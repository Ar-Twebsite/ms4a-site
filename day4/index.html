Reading Time: 5 Minutes

The History

The Medici were kicked out. Florence was a Republic again. But they were surrounded by enemies (The Pope, The French, The Medici trying to return).

The city government wanted a statue to represent their strength. They needed a symbol of a "small guy defeating a giant." They needed David.

The Problem

They had no money for marble.

There was a massive block in the cathedral yard called "The Giant." Two other sculptors had tried to carve it years ago and failed, gouging a hole in the middle. It had been sitting in the rain for 40 years. It was considered garbage.
